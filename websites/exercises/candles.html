Rescue & Emergency Care

Our dedicated team responds to calls about stray, abandoned, and injured animals across County Sligo. We provide immediate safety, shelter, and medical support when needed most.

Every animal rescued is given a second chance, thanks to our community of volunteers and supporters who make our work possible.
