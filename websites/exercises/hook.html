Hook Head Lighthouse

Explore Ireland’s Coastal Icon

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Step inside one of the oldest operational lighthouses in the world and experience centuries of maritime history. Knowledgeable guides bring the stories of shipwrecks, keepers, and the enduring beacon to life.

From the tower’s top, enjoy panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean and the rugged coastline of County Waterford, a truly breathtaking sight.

Coastal Walks

The surrounding cliffs and pathways offer spectacular walking routes. Visitors can soak in fresh sea air while spotting seabirds and marine wildlife.

Whether it’s a short stroll or a longer coastal hike, the natural beauty of the Hook Peninsula makes for an unforgettable outdoor adventure.

Visitor Centre & Café

After exploring, relax at the welcoming visitor centre, where you can learn more about the history of the lighthouse through engaging exhibits.

The café serves fresh local produce, light meals, and warm drinks, providing the perfect spot to recharge while enjoying ocean views.
